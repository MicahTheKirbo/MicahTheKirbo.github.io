Cybersafety and Digital Citizenship


Cybersafety, or being safe on the internet, is being 'safe' on the web. Safe meaning that you passwords and personal data are secure, and no malicious stuff are on your device. If you are taking the correct Cybersafety steps, it will ensure that you have a great time on the web where you don't have to worry about mal-intended strangers trying to get to your device.


Being a Digital Citizen is using the internet ethically to engage in social media, politics and other stuff responsibly. Being a digital citizen is being active on the internet, which is now important because most of our information is from the internet, like the news.




The Importance of Cybersafety


The Internet is a great invention. It allows us to search up needed information,chat with friends, get clients for your business, it seems that you can do anything with it! However, just like all things, there are also malicious things hiding on the internet. Coming across these could be very dangerous, resulting in a broken computer, data lost, or evem money stolen! Due to this, it is very important that you stay safe online, to avoid these terrible things from happening.
